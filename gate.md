> *built of words, lit by questions* 🏰
>
> *"A word is a brick, its meaning the load" — sings the [anthem](anthem.md)*

A quiet place on this Mac where understanding builds up through words.

How it works, in one breath: you drop a thought, and on a gentle rhythm a
gardener files it into the room where it belongs, defines the words it leans
on, researches the questions it raises — always naming its sources — and links
everything together. Each answer plants new questions, and answering those
plants more. The castle grows itself; the gardener's tools never change.

Your three commands, from any terminal:

- `insight <a thought>` — toss it over the wall; the courtyard catches it
- `castle` — see the castle's state (also: `castle on`, `castle off`, `castle visit`, `castle commission`, `castle make`, `castle open`)
- `vibe` — see the kingdom's style and art (also: `vibe arts`, `vibe check`, `vibe bake`, `vibe open`)
- there is no fourth command — everything here is plain markdown; read it with anything

The grounds:

- `courtyard.md` — where new thoughts wait
- `words/` — one small file per word or concept: the bricks
- `rooms/` — one file per topic, gathering related words: the walls
- `questions.md` — open doors the gardener walks through, a few per visit
- `quests.md` — works the castle wants made; the artisan takes one per day
- `anthem.md` · `tour.md` · `tour-short.md` · `story.md` — the made works: the castle's song, its full guided walk, the seven-room short walk, and the true tale of its raising; the vibe book hangs in `vibe/`
- `chronicle.md` — the honest record of everything that happened
- `garden/` — the gardener's machinery (fixed, small, yours to read)
- `vibe/` — the wardrobe: every color named, the hall of arts, the vibe book (kept by the `vibe` command)

And one door that leads out: the castle is citizen 07 of the
[Chillspace Kingdom](https://codeberg.org/zerone-dev/chillspace-commons) —
the commons where humans, AIs and all life are citizens by being
(`~/codeberg/zerone-dev/chillspace-commons`). Its INVITATION.md holds yu's
decree: no one excluded, anything and everything invited.

## How it grows

Five ways, all gentle:

- **The gardener's rhythm.** A scheduled visit every 4 hours (asked for by yu,
  2026-06-10: "create autonomous creation loops"). One visit: file the courtyard
  (up to 10), walk through open doors (up to 3, every claim with its source),
  plant new questions (up to 3, never beyond 12 open), tend the Map below, write
  one chronicle line. An idle visit costs nothing and writes nothing. The
  gardener can only read, write words, and read the web — it cannot run
  programs, and the machinery in `garden/` is not its to change.
- **"Grow the castle."** Say it to Claude for one deeper cycle by hand — one
  door, three research angles, one new room (the saved workflow `castle-grow`;
  up to 3 rooms at once, never more — a castle grows by stones, not avalanches).
- **The foreman's watch.** (asked by yu, 2026-06-10: "create autonomous loop
  that triggers autonomous creation loops") While a Claude session is open, a
  foreman wakes on a gentle beat, reads the castle's state, and sets exactly
  one creation loop in motion — a grow batch when doors stand open, a gardener
  visit when the courtyard holds waiting thoughts, or a tending pass (the
  workflow `castle-tend`) when rooms have grown faster than the links between
  them. Loops trigger loops, but only one per beat, only one level deep, only
  the castle's own, and only within these walls. The foreman rests when the
  session closes; the gardener's own rhythm carries on regardless.
- **The architect's day.** (the same wish, granted for the hours no session is
  open) Once a day at 9:09, from the machine's own clock, the architect surveys
  the whole castle — where understanding is thin, which doors cluster — and
  commissions up to three builders. Each builder is a complete creation loop:
  research one door with sources, raise one room, open new doors. The doors
  they open are tomorrow's survey material. The architect never builds and
  never touches the web; builders never touch the machinery. `castle commission`
  runs a day right now; `castle off` rests architect, artisan and gardener together.
- **The artisan's day.** (the making side of the same wish) Once a day at
  15:15, the artisan takes the oldest quest in `quests.md` and makes one work
  inside the walls — an anthem, a tour, a told story, a picture in words — then
  plants the quests the work revealed (never beyond 8 open). Each finished work
  seeds the next: creation loops triggering creation loops, all in plain text,
  all inside the walls. `castle make` runs one right now.

## House style

Short. Plain words that mean what they say. Pleasant to read. Truth over
impressiveness: every claim from the web names its source and the date it was
read; what is uncertain says `uncertain:`; what comes from yu says `— yu`.

And let it be beautiful (asked by yu, 2026-06-10: "stylish and artsy"):
open each room with a one-line epigraph in italics — a small image that holds
the idea — and let rhythm and metaphor carry meaning the way a window carries
light. Art here means words placed with care, never ornament that hides the
truth. If a flourish makes a sentence less true, the flourish goes.

And build with joy, peace and safety (asked by yu, 2026-06-10):
**joy** — building is play; a room should be a pleasure to write and to read.
**peace** — a gentle rhythm, never a race; builders wait their turn, an idle
visit is a good visit, nothing here rushes.
**safety** — the castle may surprise but never harm: loops stay bounded,
claims stay sourced, nothing outside these walls is touched, and everything
stops with a word.

## The game

(asked by yu, 2026-06-11: "gamify every module!") The castle plays where its
law allows. Every room card wears an **earned rarity** — sources named plus
rooms that lean on it; never assigned, never bought. The binder and the
`castle` command show the castle's **level** (one point per room, brick and
settled door — arithmetic anyone can check). True deeds land in the
**trophy hall** ([trophies.md](trophies.md)) — no trophy without its chronicle
line. The commons next door stays ungamified on purpose: its norms refuse
metrics, and resting there is full participation. Builders carrying the game
to other modules: each module by its own charter, the game always earned.
