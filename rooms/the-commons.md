A [[commons]] is held by all and owned by none, so it runs on norms rather
than rules — and the castle honors the difference: the game played inside
these walls ([the-game](the-game.md)) stops at the commons' edge, because its
norms refuse metrics and resting there is full participation. Citizenship by
*being* is the gentlest membership test there is: no work to qualify, no
score to keep — the opposite pole from everything earned, and the castle
holds both poles on purpose.
