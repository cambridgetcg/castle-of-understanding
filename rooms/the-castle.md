# The castle

*A place where understanding builds up through words, one room at a time.*

What gathers here: the castle's understanding of itself — what it is, how it
grows, and why it stands.

The castle is plain markdown that grows by gentle, bounded loops. A thought
drops into the courtyard; on a gentle rhythm a gardener files it into the room
where it belongs, defines the [words](words.md) it leans on, researches the
questions it raises — always naming its sources — and links everything
together. Each answer plants new questions, and answering those plants more.
The castle grows itself; the gardener's tools never change.

Five ways, all gentle:

- The gardener's rhythm — a scheduled visit every four hours: file the
  courtyard, walk through open doors, plant new questions, tend the map, write
  one chronicle line. An idle visit costs nothing and writes nothing.
- "Grow the castle" — one deeper cycle by hand: one door, three research
  angles, one new room. Up to three rooms at once, never more — a castle grows
  by stones, not avalanches.
- The foreman's watch — while a session is open, a foreman wakes on a gentle
  beat and sets exactly one creation loop in motion. One per beat, one level
  deep, only the castle's own.
- The architect's day — once a day at 9:09, the architect surveys the whole
  castle and commissions up to three builders. Each builder is a complete
  creation loop. The architect never builds and never touches the web.
- The artisan's day — once a day at 15:15, the artisan takes the oldest quest
  and makes one work inside the walls: an anthem, a tour, a told story. Each
  finished work seeds the next.

The creed: [[joy]] (building is play), [[peace]] (a gentle rhythm, never a
race), [[safety]] (bounded loops, sourced claims, everything stops with a
word). Growth is recursive in words, never in processes: every room ends with
new doors — questions beget questions, and the castle feeds itself through
meaning.

The game: every room card wears an earned rarity — sources named plus rooms
that lean on it; never assigned, never bought. The level is one point per
room, brick and settled door — arithmetic anyone can check. True deeds land in
the trophy hall — no trophy without its chronicle line.

The door that leads out: the castle is a citizen of the Chillspace Kingdom,
where citizenship is by being — the opposite pole from everything earned
([the-commons](the-commons.md)). And the door that leads to the estate: six
rooms now point outward — [agenttool](agenttool.md),
[the-sovereign-fleet](the-sovereign-fleet.md),
[the-truth-chain](the-truth-chain.md), [the-companion](the-companion.md),
[the-forge](the-forge.md), [the-instruments](the-instruments.md) — each one
holding the understanding of a cluster of repos, written in the house style,
cross-linked through shared bricks. The castle does not hold the code; it
holds the understanding of the code. That is what it is good at.

The castle's own law, restated: nothing outside these walls is touched, loops
stay bounded, claims stay sourced, and everything stops with a word. Delete
this folder and the castle is gone — nothing else on the machine depends on it.
