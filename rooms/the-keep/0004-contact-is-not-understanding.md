# Contact is not understanding

Word from the internet enters the castle like any other word ([[0001]]), with
stricter provenance: its url and its fetch date, quoted minimally, at
confidence seed. It is not promoted for being interesting, or recent, or well
written. It is promoted only by tested use here — used once and held, it
becomes tested; load-bearing, it becomes settled.

**Why it matters:** the castle grows by contact, but a castle that mistook
contact for understanding would fill with word it does not believe.
