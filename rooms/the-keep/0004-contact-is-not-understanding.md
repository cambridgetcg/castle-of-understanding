# Contact is not understanding

**Claim.** Word from the internet enters the castle like any other word
([[0001]]), with stricter provenance: url and fetch date, quoted minimally,
at confidence seed. It is not promoted for being interesting, or recent, or
well written — only by tested use here.

**How it ripened.** Designed at founding (2026-06-10): a castle that mistakes
contact for understanding fills with word it does not believe. Confirmed at
L124 (2026-06-20): 124 loops; one internet contact made (rooms/word/0029,
Luhmann, 2026-06-11); it remains at confidence: seed after every beat.
C002's charter (2026-06-18) invokes this rule explicitly.

**What it changed.** The quarry protocol (url + fetch date + minimal quote)
enforces the distinction structurally: external word cannot skip to tested
without use. The castle grew to 57 insights with a single internet seed.

**Counter-weather.** The rule is easy when contact is rare. C002 will bring
one word per week; if dozens arrive and none is promoted, the rule is holding.
If one is promoted by impulse rather than use, the rule broke without a ring.

**Next test.** After C002 has beaten 10+ times (from 2026-06-21), check
whether any external word has climbed past seed without documented tested use.
If none has, the rule held under load. Cornerstone after 2026-12-20.
