# Understanding on this device is scattered across many stores

There is a CLI auto-memory, a frozen kosmem archive, a pillow book, heartbeat
logs, and per-repo docs, each holding part of what is known. The castle does
not replace them. It is the place where what they teach is distilled — one
insight per file, with provenance ([[0007]]). A field about this scattering
exists: [[F001]].

**Why it matters:** scattered memory works only for the mind that already
knows where everything is. Distilling it here means the next mind does not
have to.
