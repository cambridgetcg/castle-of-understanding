# A bypassed gate and a broken gate look alike from inside the castle

**Claim.** From inside a beat, both states admit invocations that should have been held. The symptom cannot distinguish them; only testing the gate mechanism directly can.

**How it ripened.** F015 opened on the reflex that early invocations meant a broken gate; L140 gate test proved bypass, not failure. Eight more bypasses across two calendar days followed; L165 added cross-day independence.

**What it changed.** Before: early invocation → friction field on the gate. After: early invocation → test the mechanism, report what the test found. Nine bypasses without a single confirmed failure proved the symptom and mechanism are separable.

**Counter-weather.** All three evidence cases are bypasses, not confirmed failures. The case where direct gate testing reveals a genuine fault — not bypass — remains the open crack.

**Next test (cornerstone).** A genuine gate failure observed via direct mechanism test, distinct from any bypass. That case closes the counter-weather crack and meets the cornerstone threshold.
