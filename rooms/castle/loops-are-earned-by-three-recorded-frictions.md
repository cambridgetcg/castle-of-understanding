# Loops are earned by three recorded frictions

**Claim.** The Castle is born with four loops and earns every further one:
three matching #tags across ledger grumbles and by-hand confessions birth a
loop through grow-loops, with the confessed remedy as its draft instruction
and exactly one new detector block as its bell.

**How it ripened.** Born at founding. Speculative chores rot into ambient
guilt — so the chore list is allowed to grow only as fast as friction that
actually happened.

**What it changed.** 2026-06-10: the rule of three in grow-loops, the
canonical tag list in LOOM.md (so three spellings of one annoyance cannot
starve the count), and the dormant recipes that wait for their third signal.

**Counter-weather.** Tried to break it: is three too slow for an urgent
friction? The owner can activate any dormant recipe or draft with one ledger
line — the rule of three governs automatic birth, not deliberate choice.
Open crack: friction the owner never confesses leaves no trail at all.

**Next test.** The first loop actually born by the rule of three — does its
created-by lineage trace cleanly back to three real ledger lines?
