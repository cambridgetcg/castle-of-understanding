# Adaptive fading drops one scaffold step at a time as a tutor verifies each — can a learner alone run their own fading honestly, when fog-meter found the self-read so weak?

*Alone on the scaffold, you do not ask yourself whether the wall can stand —
you take one plank away, lay the next course bare-handed, and hold it to the
plumb line.*

Split the tutor's verdict into its jobs and the question answers itself
unevenly. The tutor does three things: chooses which step to bare next,
witnesses the bare attempt, and grades it. Handed all three at once, learners
do them badly — a meta-analysis of learner control in educational technology
found an overall effect near zero (g = 0.05; read 2026-06-10 —
[Karich, Burns & Maki 2014](https://eric.ed.gov/?id=EJ1034262)); in real
tutoring systems, 72% of help actions were unproductive, split between
clicking to the bottom-out hint and refusing help while drowning (read
2026-06-10 — [Aleven et al., Toward Tutoring Help Seeking](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~bmclaren/pubs/AlevenEtAl-HelpSeeking-ITS2004.pdf));
and left to grade their own recall, students scored wrong answers as right,
dropped items too early, and measurably learned less for it (read 2026-06-10 —
[Dunlosky & Rawson 2012](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0959475211000685)).

But the jobs differ. The first two survive solitude. The cover test of
[when-the-trade-flips](when-the-trade-flips.md) *is* the tutor's own
criterion — a step fades once produced without help (read 2026-06-10 —
[Salden et al.](http://www.cee.uma.pt/ron/Salden%20et%20al.%20-%20The%20Expertise%20Reversal%20Effect%20and%20Worked%20Examples.pdf))
— self-administered. Production is the honest part. What rots alone is the
grading, and the research names the rot precisely: *absence of standards*.
Learners judging their answers while inspecting the correct one judged far
more accurately, and breaking the standard into idea units and checking each
one cut the overconfidence on wrong-but-close answers (read 2026-06-10 —
[Dunlosky, Hartwig, Rawson & Lipko 2011](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1080/17470218.2010.502239)).
Judge late, besides — delayed self-judgments discriminate known from unknown
far better than judgments made in the warmth of study (read 2026-06-10 —
[Rhodes & Tauber 2011](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/21219059/)).

So the solo learner never replaces the tutor's judgment with the inner feel
[fog-meter](fog-meter.md) discredited. They replace it with the tutor's
*procedure* plus the tutor's answer pressed into paper: cover one step,
attempt it bare, compare the attempt to the worked solution piece by piece,
and let a rule — not a vote — decide. Matched the key: that plank comes down.
Failed: the plank goes back, no debate. It is fog-meter's predict-then-check
ledger turned to [[scaffolding]], and the borrowed calibration of
[training-the-eye](training-the-eye.md) by another name. Even running this
protocol is trainable — students who watched a model self-assess and pick the
next task got better at both, and learned more (read 2026-06-10 —
[Kostons, van Gog & Paas 2012](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0959475211000697)).

Yes, then — alone, but only as a clerk, never as a judge. Honesty is
outsourced to the key and the rule; the feel is never consulted, only
trained on the side.

## What stays uncertain

uncertain: no study found hands learners control of the *fading itself* and
measures whether they run it honestly — the near-zero meta covers learner
control broadly, and the idea-unit and ledger fixes are lab-scale and short.
Whether rule-following survives weeks of solo motivation, with the clerk and
the learner one tired person, is untested. And the whole protocol presupposes
a worked solution to grade against — where no key exists, this answer stops
and [standard-without-a-key](standard-without-a-key.md) carries on: the
standard is made or borrowed, criteria pressed concrete, an admired exemplar
rebuilt, judgments pooled from many eyes. The nearest test kept in this room
is [reread-or-refamiliar](reread-or-refamiliar.md)'s: step away and let delayed
blank-page generation judge what no key can.
