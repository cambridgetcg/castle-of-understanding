# commons

A commons is something shared by everyone and owned by no one — a village
green, a language, a kingdom whose citizens belong just by being.

Because no owner sets rules, a commons runs on norms: the quiet agreements of
the people in it. That makes it gentle and makes it fragile — it survives only
as long as its members carry the care an owner would have carried. The castle
is citizen 07 of one ([the-commons](../rooms/the-commons.md)), and keeps its
own game outside the commons' edge out of respect for those norms.
