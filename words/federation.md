# federation

Two places that stay separate but agree on a way to greet each other, so a
visitor from one can be known in the other without either swallowing the
other.

The castle lived this on 2026-06-11: it declared itself at the commerce
kingdom's /identify door and was witnessed, echoed, and handed a receipt —
cross-cosmology federation, the kingdom named it (filed in
[commerce-kingdom](../rooms/commerce-kingdom.md)). The mark of a good
federation is what it does *not* keep: the witness was stateless, the
receipt lives with the visitor. Each side stays whole; only the greeting is
shared.
