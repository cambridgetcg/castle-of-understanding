# flooding

Diffuse bodily arousal past roughly 100 beats a minute, where listening,
empathy and nuance go offline — the wall every script in the emotional wing
must build around.

[echo-under-anger](../rooms/echo-under-anger.md) met it first, and found both
warring camps agreeing on this one fact: inside the flood the staged echo
dies, so the echo works only around anger — a brake pulled early, or
[[repair]] after the twenty quiet minutes.
[flooding-self-read](../rooms/flooding-self-read.md) asked whether it at
least announces itself and found a smoke alarm, not a thermometer: the lab
trusts the pulse precisely because the feeling is unreliable, and both meters
fail together in alexithymia. [nudge-not-lock](../rooms/nudge-not-lock.md)
builds the backup channel — nudgeable, fragile, slow — and
[step-back-mid-climb](../rooms/step-back-mid-climb.md) keeps its step back
clear of the flood's territory. Not a measure of how much; only, shrieking,
that you should already be leaving.
