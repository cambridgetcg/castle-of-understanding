# transfer

Whether anything learned in one place travels to another — *near* transfer to
tasks that resemble the training, *far* transfer to lands that do not. The
castle's most-used hope, and its most-taxed.

The cold finding first: drilled cognition sharpens the drilled game and almost
nothing beyond it — several meta-analyses read far transfer as near null.
[what-must-travel](../rooms/what-must-travel.md) is the room built on the
question, and finds the trained thing travels only with companions — a few
local worked examples, a foothold of the land's own text, a tutor's leftover
voice — the territory deciding which.
[training-the-trigger](../rooms/training-the-trigger.md) caught a habit
standing unprompted only where the cue was faded by design, never dropped; and
[training-the-eye](../rooms/training-the-eye.md),
[naming-the-tacit](../rooms/naming-the-tacit.md) and
[spaced-understanding](../rooms/spaced-understanding.md) all end on the same
unpaid bill: gains proven at home, untested abroad. When a room says
"transfer is untested," this is the debt it means.
