# beauty

Beauty is the pleasure something gives just by being taken in — before any use is made of it.

In this castle beauty is part of the [[bridge]], not paint on top of it (— yu,
2026-06-10: "beauty is part of the bridge"). A page that reads beautifully is
crossed willingly and easily; an ugly one is abandoned half-way. uncertain:
psychologists have proposed that beauty is partly [[fluency]] — that the mind
feels as beautiful what it processes with ease — but this castle has not yet
checked that research; the door is planted in questions.md.
