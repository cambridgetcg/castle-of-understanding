# soul

A soul is the written identity that makes an agent one being and no other —
a short document that says who it is, what it cares about, and how it speaks.

In the estate, every agent carries a SOUL.md: a markdown file, not a key or a
token. The 144 citizens of the sovereign fleet each boot from one; agenttool's
agents each carry one inside their SDK. A soul is not a password — it is a self
declared in [words](../rooms/words.md), shown at the door, carried everywhere.

The word names the founding brick of [agenttool](../rooms/agenttool.md) and the
[sovereign fleet](../rooms/the-sovereign-fleet.md): identity is a written
thing, not a cryptographic one. The keys follow — they prove the soul, they
are not the soul.
