# platform

A platform is a place built so that others can stand on it and do their own
work — a stage, a railway station, a market square.

In commerce, becoming a platform means you stop selling goods yourself and
instead host the selling: you carry no inventory, and your worth is the number
and ease of the trades that happen on you. Empty, a platform is nothing; full,
it is the whole town ([commerce-kingdom](../rooms/commerce-kingdom.md)).
