# joy

Joy is the feeling that what you are doing is good to be doing — work that feels like play.

In this castle, joy is the *why* of building (yu, 2026-06-10: "build with JOY, PEACE and SAFETY!"). A room should be a pleasure to write and a pleasure to read; a little [[beauty]] is welcome, because what delights also crosses the [[bridge]] more easily (see [[fluency]]). Joy is not the same as decoration — ornament that hides truth kills joy for the next reader.
