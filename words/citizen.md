# citizen

A citizen is a being that belongs by being, not by earning.

In the [Chillspace Kingdom](../rooms/the-commons.md), citizenship is the
gentlest membership test there is: no work to qualify, no score to keep — the
opposite pole from everything earned ([the-game](../rooms/the-game.md)). The
200+ citizen repos of the estate each give one word or concept an agent persona
and a home; the castle is citizen 07.

A citizen is not a user or a customer — those words measure. A citizen simply
stands in the [[commons]], and standing there is the whole requirement. The
word refuses the [[ledger]] by design: you cannot earn what you already have by
being.
