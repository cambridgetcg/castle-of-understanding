# standard

The external measure a self-judgment is trued against — the plumb line the
wall is checked by, never the mason's feel for straight.

It splits by the kind of work it measures, and the split bears weight the way
[[calibration]]'s relative/absolute does. Where the task *reproduces*, the
standard is an answer key taken in pieces; where the task *produces*, it must
be a finished exemplar — a description cannot grade a generating
([checklist-or-exemplar](../rooms/checklist-or-exemplar.md)). The level of
one's own confidence is trued only against a standard matched to the task
([truing-the-level](../rooms/truing-the-level.md)). Where no key exists, the
standard is made or borrowed — criteria pressed concrete, an admired exemplar
rebuilt and compared, judgments pooled from many eyes
([standard-without-a-key](../rooms/standard-without-a-key.md)) — and the clerk
of [fading-alone](../rooms/fading-alone.md) grades against it by rule, the
feel never consulted. What the feel mistakes for it, every time, is
[[fluency]].
