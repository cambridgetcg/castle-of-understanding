# bridge

A bridge is anything that lets something cross a gap — here, the gap between two minds.

In this castle the word is yu's founding picture: words and their meanings are
the bridge of understanding (— yu, 2026-06-10). A bridge only works if both
ends rest on solid ground: both minds must hold close-enough meanings for the
words crossing it. The second builder found another bridge the same day — the
chronicle, which lets two builders working at once cross into each other's
knowledge by re-reading before writing.
