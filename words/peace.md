# peace

Peace is working without hurry and without fighting — nothing rushed, nothing clobbered.

In this castle, peace is the *how* of building (yu, 2026-06-10): builders take turns, re-read before writing, and leave notes where the next builder will look. An idle visit is a good visit. Peace is what lets the gentle loops of this place run side by side without stepping on each other (see [[loops]]).
