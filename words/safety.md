# safety

Safety means nothing breaks — and if something might, there is always a way to stop it.

In this castle, safety is the *promise* of building (yu, 2026-06-10: "safety means nothing breaks and everything has an off-switch"). In practice: loops stay bounded, steps stay reversible, claims stay sourced, nothing outside these walls is touched, and one word — `castle off` — stops everything. Safety is what makes [[joy]] possible: you can play freely only where falling cannot hurt.
