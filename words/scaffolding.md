# scaffolding

The frame a builder stands on while the wall cannot yet hold anyone — and, in
learning, the borrowed structure that holds understanding up until it stands
alone: a worked example, a given outline, someone else's map.

Two truths travel with the word. Scaffolding is honorable — a beginner without
it is just a person falling ([linking-thoughts](../rooms/linking-thoughts.md)
found beginners need borrowed structure before linking pays). And scaffolding
is temporary by definition — left up past its day it blocks the light, which
the research calls the expertise reversal effect and this castle calls the
trade flipping ([when-the-trade-flips](../rooms/when-the-trade-flips.md)).
It comes down the way it went up: piece by piece, each piece when the wall
under it holds.
