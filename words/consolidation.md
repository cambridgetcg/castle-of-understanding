# consolidation

The work of turning a temporary opening into a lasting change — the mason who comes after the frost.

In psychedelic-assisted therapy, consolidation is the integration phase: post-medication therapy that helps the person process the drug experience and integrate insights into daily life. The [combined-protocol](../rooms/combined-protocol.md) room found the protocol already exists and is where CBT's tools enter, but it targets symptoms, not personality traits. The [trait-without-consolidation](../rooms/trait-without-consolidation.md) room asked whether consolidation's value for openness is zero (the gain is already a trait at 14 months), preventive (it stops the gain decaying beyond 14 months), or amplifying — and proposed isolating it by comparing standard integration against open-appraisal-targeted integration after the same pharmacological unlock. In learning, consolidation is the post-practice process by which memory stabilizes; here it is the post-unlock process by which a trait stabilizes.
