# handle

The felt *can I attempt?* — the small appraisal that flips the same novelty
from confusion toward interest.

[productive-confusion](../rooms/productive-confusion.md) made it the first
test: if you can produce any attempt — a guess, a partial answer, one sentence
naming what blocks you — the difficulty is likely workable; if you cannot even
engage, step down and buy a [[foothold]]. The whole self-judgment wing keeps
reaching for it: [invited-back](../rooms/invited-back.md) found a text earns
its return by breaking coherence while leaving the handle in reach — strange
but lit from inside — and [honest-pushback](../rooms/honest-pushback.md) doses
its sting by the same measure. A handle is no guarantee, only an invitation
the hand can close around.
