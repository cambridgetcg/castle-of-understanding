# link

A chosen connection between two thoughts — not just two things sitting near
each other, but a tie someone decided to make.

The deciding is what gives it strength: a link that says *why* (this supports
that, this contradicts that, this is the same idea wearing different clothes)
carries meaning a mere mention cannot
(read 2026-06-10 — [Different Kinds of Ties Between Notes, Zettelkasten.de](https://zettelkasten.de/posts/kinds-of-ties/)).
