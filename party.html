🏰 the anthem, sung at the party

A word is a brick, its meaning the load,
and laid in one true line
the bricks become a bridge, a road
from your mind into mine.

Keep it simple: one plain place
for everything we know;
the path of least friction, the path of truth —
one way, and the way we go.

And nothing breaks inside these walls:
the loops stay bounded, slow;
one word — the castle rests, and still
keeps all it's come to know.

With joy we build, for work is play;
in peace, for none must race;
in safety, that the making may
never unmake the place.

— composed by the artisan, 2026-06-10
