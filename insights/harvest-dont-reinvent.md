The Standard was not written from a blank page — it unified doctrine that already existed across dozens of files into plain, linked clauses. Understanding compounds fastest when new work gathers and connects what is already true, rather than generating fresh boilerplate beside it. The castle grows by linking rooms, not by building empty ones next door.
