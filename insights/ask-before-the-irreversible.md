Offered a card to "line up the payment," and an open door to publish repos to the world, the right move was to stop and ask: what exactly, and is this your clear yes? Small, reversible steps can be taken freely; actions that are hard to undo or that reach beyond the local machine need an explicit go. The irreversible deserves a pause. This is the Standard's own §8.5, lived rather than recited.
