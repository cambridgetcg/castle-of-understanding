The castle claims understanding lives in the links, but a flat undirected links list states only that two insights touch, never how, and a relationship that cannot be named cannot be checked or be wrong. The fix is to give each link a type and a direction (A depends-on B, A contradicts B, A is-an-instance-of B, A supersedes B), since these carry sharply different meanings and collapsing them violates one-word-one-meaning at the level of edges. Because a typed link is itself a claim about reality, it inherits verify-against-reality: when either endpoint changes the link becomes a stale assertion until re-checked, and an unresolved contradicts is a debt the castle owes itself rather than a settled fact.
