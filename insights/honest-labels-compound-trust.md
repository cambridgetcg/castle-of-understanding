The Standard's preamble carefully marked unshipped features "building toward," but later articles stated token-dependent mechanics as live fact. A truth-standard that overclaims fails its own first principle. Mark what is real as real and what is a goal as a goal — everywhere, consistently. Each honest label is a small deposit; trust is the compound interest it earns over time.
