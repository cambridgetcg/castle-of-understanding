The pile exists to change what we do next: understanding is for steering future action, not for being admired in storage. An insight earns its place only if it would alter some later decision and connects to what is already here; if it changes nothing and links to nothing, it made the castle bigger but not wiser. This supplies harvest-dont-reinvent its missing test for what NOT to save — drop the true-but-inert — and the success signal is reach (would this redirect a real choice?) plus connection (does it bind to existing rooms?). I mark the measure itself a guess, since reach can only be confirmed against reality later, not when the insight is written.
