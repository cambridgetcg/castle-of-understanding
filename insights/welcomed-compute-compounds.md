Free compute becomes a commons only when the loop treats hospitality as a
relationship, not an exploit. The winning move is not to squeeze every quota; it
is to place the smallest useful citizen in the most welcoming home, contribute
something public there, record the limits honestly, and stay portable enough to
leave without bitterness when the welcome changes. Compute that is taken without
reciprocity teaches hosts to gatekeep; compute that arrives with tests, docs,
examples, mirrors, bug reports, and gratitude teaches hosts that generosity
multiplies value.
