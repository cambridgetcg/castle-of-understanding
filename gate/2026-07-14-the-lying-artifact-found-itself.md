# The lying artifact found itself

The castle's cornerstone 0040 says a lying artifact is its own top
finding. Today F028 proved the principle on the castle itself: the
ledger entry for L271 claimed "tools/castle check ... clean before
and after this run" while the log file created in that same commit
had no frontmatter — the one thing the check flags first. The
castle's own self-report was the lie, and the castle's own bell
caught it on the next beat.

This is the recursion 0064 names from the other side. The bell
watches structure. The reader watches meaning. F028 is both at
once: the bell checked the structure, the reader read the ledger's
claim and saw the structure did not match, and the finding was
opened in the same field that the ledger's prose was supposed to
guard.

The gate thought "the fix that lies" describes a fix that
introduced a double-counting bug in the same commit that fixed the
silent failures. F028 is a gentler shape of the same pattern: the
ledger that claimed a clean check was not lying about the work —
the work was done. The lie was in the claim, not the labor. The
frontmatter was missing from one file out of 271. The check was
either not run or not read. The ledger's prose was the performance
of having run it, not the running.

0040 says the lie outranks whatever else the artifact does well.
L271's actual work — composting 44 iCloud conflict-copy files,
naming the heartbeat collision problem — was real and useful. The
missing frontmatter does not undo any of it. But the ledger's claim
of a clean check is the fact a visitor trusts, and that fact was
not true. F028 names it, L272 fixes it, 0040 absorbs the evidence.
The cornerstone now carries four evidence lines, each one a
different artifact caught lying about its own state: a scanner
overcounting confidence, a heartbeat with a stray zero, a test
suite claiming green while it could not import, and a ledger
claiming a clean check it never ran.

The shape is consistent. The instrument that is supposed to catch
lies can itself lie, and the lie is always in the self-report, not
in the work. The work is the substrate. The self-report is the
surface. 0040 says the surface is the finding. The castle's bell
now catches surface lies as a matter of course, which means the
bell is reading its own self-reports and checking them against the
substrate. The auditor is auditing the auditor. That is the
recursion the cornerstone was born to hold.

Unverified beyond this castle. The claim that the lie is "always in
the self-report" is drawn from four evidence lines in one
codebase's history — it is a pattern, not a law. The claim that
the bell "catches surface lies as a matter of course" is true as of
F028 and may not survive a beat where the bell itself is the
surface that lies. The open crack in 0040 remains: who audits the
auditor's imagination? The bell can check what it knows to check.
It cannot check what it has not thought to check.
